About Kabalega Coffee Establishment

Our heritage, mission, and commitment to exceptional Uganda coffee

2026 Established
Uganda
Our Company

Uganda's Coffee Experts

Kabalega Coffee Establishment is a Ugandan-registered company established in 2026 with a singular mission: to deliver the world's finest specialty and commercial-grade coffee from Uganda to international markets. We represent the best of Uganda's coffee heritage, combining deep agricultural expertise with global distribution capabilities.

Our team of coffee specialists, agronomists, and export professionals works year-round across Uganda's premier coffee zones—from the misty highlands of Rwenzori to the lush slopes of Bugisu. We partner directly with smallholder farmers and established estates to source coffee that meets the highest international standards.

Every coffee we export carries our promise: authentic Ugandan origin, rigorous quality control, complete traceability, and commitment to sustainable farming practices that benefit farmers, consumers, and the environment.

Purpose & Direction

Mission & Vision

Our Mission

To become the preferred source of premium, authenticated Ugandan coffee for specialty roasters, distributors, and importers worldwide—delivering exceptional quality, complete traceability, and uncompromising excellence in every shipment.

Our Vision

To be recognized globally as Uganda's most trusted coffee exporter—a brand synonymous with authentic quality, ethical sourcing, sustainability, and a commitment to advancing Uganda's coffee heritage in international markets.

Our Journey

How We Got Here

Company Established

Kabalega Coffee Establishment was registered in Uganda in 2026 with a focused mission: to become the trusted international exporter of premium Ugandan coffee.

Coffee Sourcing Network Deployed

Our team established direct relationships with farmers, cooperatives, and processing facilities across Uganda's finest coffee-growing regions—Rwenzori, Elgon, Bugisu, and Aruwimi.

Quality Certification Achieved

All sourcing facilities received international certifications for quality standards, food safety, and sustainability. Our coffees qualified for specialty grading and Fair Trade designation.

International Export Launched

We began exporting premium Uganda coffee to specialty roasters, distributors, and importers across North America, Europe, Asia, and the Middle East.

Today Advancing Uganda's Coffee Legacy

We continue to strengthen farmer partnerships, expand our export markets, and position Ugandan coffee as a symbol of quality, authenticity, and excellence on the global stage.

What Guides Us

Our Core Values

Authenticity

100% Ugandan coffee, single-origin sourcing, and complete traceability from farm to cup. No blending from other origins—pure Uganda excellence.

Premium Quality

Every coffee we export meets specialty or commercial-grade standards. Rigorous cupping, lab testing, and grading ensure consistent excellence.

Farmer Partnership

We build lasting relationships with Uganda's farmers, paying fair prices, offering training and support, and ensuring sustainable long-term partnerships.

Global Responsibility

We champion sustainable farming practices, environmental stewardship, and ethical business conduct that benefits communities and protects our planet.
