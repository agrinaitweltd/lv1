Our Coffee Sourcing

Direct procurement from Uganda's finest coffee estates and cooperatives with complete traceability and sustainability

Our Origin

Uganda: The Coffee Heartland

Uganda is home to some of the world's most renowned coffee-growing regions. From the misty highlands of Mount Rwenzori to the fertile slopes of Mount Elgon and the lush Bugisu region, Uganda produces both exceptional Arabica and world-class Robusta. The country's equatorial climate, volcanic soils, and altitude create ideal conditions for growing coffee of remarkable quality and complexity.

Kabalega Coffee Establishment works exclusively with Uganda's coffee communities, smallholder farmers, cooperatives, and established estates. We know our suppliers personally, understand their farming practices, and maintain long-term partnerships that reward quality and support sustainable livelihoods.

Step by Step

How We Source

1

Identify & Engage Coffee Producers

We locate farms, cooperatives, and wet mills in Uganda's key coffee zones with the right varieties, certifications, and quality standards for our buyers.

2

Cupping & Quality Assessment

Coffee samples are professionally cupped and evaluated for aroma, flavor profile, body, acidity, and defects. We match lots to buyer specifications and quality requirements.

3

Processing & Milling

Coffee is processed using washed or natural methods, then milled and sorted at certified facilities. Grading by screen size, defect count, and quality is performed according to international standards.

4

Quality Verification & Testing

Independent lab analysis confirms moisture content, defect levels, and coffee safety. Samples are re-cupped to verify consistency and quality standards.

5

Certification & Documentation

All required export documentation is prepared: phytosanitary certificates, certificate of origin, fumigation reports, weight notes, and full traceability documentation.

6

Export & Delivery

Coffee is carefully packed in premium packaging and shipped via sea or air freight. We provide tracking and support through customs clearance until final delivery.

Regions

Key Sourcing Regions

Uganda

Our primary sourcing hub. Uganda provides Robusta and Arabica coffee, vanilla, sesame, chia seeds, dried fruits, fresh produce in large volumes and excellent quality.

Kenya

Premium Arabica coffee from Nyeri, Kirinyaga, and Kiambu. Avocados, French beans, macadamia, mangoes, and passion fruit sourced from Kenyan highlands and sub-tropical zones.

Tanzania

Arabica coffee from Kilimanjaro and Mbeya regions, cashew nuts from the south, sesame from the central corridor, and assorted fresh produce year-round.

Expanding Network

We continuously evaluate new sourcing partnerships in neighbouring countries including Rwanda, Ethiopia, and Mozambique to diversify our supply base.
