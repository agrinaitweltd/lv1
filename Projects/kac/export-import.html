International Trade

Exporting to International Markets

Beyond the UK, Axis International has the capability and network to export agricultural produce to buyers around the world. Whether you are an importer in Europe, the Middle East, Asia, or the Americas we can coordinate a shipment to suit your requirements.

We handle all export documentation from the source country, including phytosanitary certificates, commercial invoices, bills of lading, fumigation reports, and certificates of origin.

What We Handle

Our Trade Services

Documentation & Compliance

We manage all required export and import documentation including phytosanitary certificates, certificates of origin, commercial invoices, and packing lists.

Freight & Logistics

End-to-end freight coordination via sea (FCL/LCL) and air. We work with trusted freight forwarders to deliver on time and within budget.

Quality Assurance

Pre-shipment inspections, laboratory testing, and grading at source ensure every consignment meets buyer specifications and destination market standards.

Trade Finance Support

We work flexibly with buyers on payment terms and can accommodate letters of credit (LC), documentary collections, and other trade finance instruments.

Custom Packaging

Private labelling and custom packaging available for buyers who wish to brand produce under their own label for retail or wholesale distribution.

Long-Term Supply Contracts

We offer seasonal and annual supply agreements to buyers who require consistent, planned volumes with pricing stability and supply guarantees.
