Other Produce

Vanilla, spices, nuts, and specialty agricultural products from East Africa

Specialty Produce

Beyond Coffee, Fruit & Grain

East Africa produces a wide range of high-value agricultural products beyond the major commodity categories. Axis International sources and exports vanilla, spices, nuts, and other specialty produce connecting niche producers with international buyers.

We work closely with farming cooperatives and processors to source these products at the right quality, volume, and price point for our buyers.

Range

Specialty Products

Vanilla

Grade A cured vanilla beans from Uganda one of the world's top vanilla-producing countries. Rich in vanillin content with a smooth, creamy flavour profile. Available in various lengths and grades.

Macadamia Nuts

Premium raw and roasted macadamia nuts from Kenya. Whole kernels, halves, and pieces. Excellent oil content and flavour, processed to international food safety standards.

Cashew Nuts

Raw cashew nuts (RCN) and processed kernels from Tanzania. Graded W180, W240, W320, and splits. Tanzania is a significant cashew-producing origin in East Africa.

Chilli & Spices

Bird's eye chilli, dried ginger, turmeric, and black pepper from Uganda and Tanzania. Cleaned, dried, and packed. Increasingly in demand across European and Asian spice markets.

Shea Butter

Unrefined and refined shea butter from Uganda. Used in cosmetics, skincare, and food manufacturing. Available in bulk drums or custom-packed for retail-ready supply.

Moringa

Moringa leaf powder, dried leaves, and moringa oil. Sourced from Uganda. High demand from health supplement, food, and cosmetics industries worldwide.

Industries

Who Buys Specialty Produce?

Cosmetics & Skincare

Shea butter, moringa oil, and vanilla extract are in high demand from natural beauty and skincare brands.

Food & Flavouring

Premium vanilla, chillies, and spices are used by food manufacturers, bakeries, ice cream makers, and flavour houses.

Health Supplements

Moringa powder, cashew nuts, and macadamia are increasingly sourced by supplement brands and health food retailers.

Snack & Nut Trade

Cashew and macadamia kernels are supplied to snack brands, retailers, and nut processors across Europe and the Middle East.
