Our Premium Coffee

100% Ugandan specialty and commercial-grade coffee for roasters and distributors worldwide

Premium Coffee

Authentic Ugandan Coffee Excellence

Uganda ranks among the world's top coffee producers, renowned for exceptional Robusta and high-altitude Arabica. Kabalega Coffee Establishment sources exclusively from Uganda's premier coffee zones—Rwenzori, Elgon, Bugisu, and Aruwimi—delivering specialty and commercial-grade coffee to roasters and importers worldwide.

We supply green (unroasted) coffee beans in multiple grades, screen sizes, and processing methods. Whether you need specialty micro-lots with full traceability or consistent commercial volumes, we deliver superior quality with complete documentation and reliable logistics.

Coffee Types

Our Uganda Coffee Selection

Specialty Arabica

High-altitude Arabica from Mount Rwenzori and Elgon regions. Bright acidity, complex fruit and floral notes, excellent for specialty roasting. Washed and natural process varieties.

Commercial Robusta

Full-bodied Robusta from Bugisu and western Uganda estates. Screen 15+ and Screen 18 FAQ grades. Ideal for espresso blends, instant coffee, and consistent commercial supply.

Single-Origin Lots

Traceable micro-lots with cupping notes and direct farm documentation. Perfect for specialty roasters seeking authentic Uganda coffee with complete provenance and story.

Standards

Quality & Export Specifications

Cupping & Grading

All coffee undergoes professional cupping and grading by screen size, moisture, defect count, and quality score. Electronic sorting and hand-sorting ensure consistency.

Export Packaging

Standard 60kg GrainPro-lined jute bags for long-distance shipping. Custom packaging available for branded or specialty orders. Vacuum sealing options available.

Certifications Available

Fair Trade, Organic, Rainforest Alliance, and direct-origin certification available. Full traceability documentation for all shipments with farm-level details.

Shipping & Logistics

Full Container Load (FCL) sea freight from Mombasa or Dar es Salaam. LCL, air freight, and express options available. Door-to-door delivery with tracking and insurance.
