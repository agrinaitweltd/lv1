Who We Are

Uganda's Premier Coffee Exporter

Kabalega Coffee Establishment is a Ugandan-registered company dedicated to exporting the world's finest specialty and commercial-grade coffee. We source from Uganda's most prestigious coffee-growing regions and deliver to discerning buyers across North America, Europe, Asia, and the Middle East.

Uganda's Coffee Heritage
